Ask a Question
How long does a website project take?

Most projects are completed within 2–4 weeks. Landing pages typically take 5–7 business days, full websites 2–4 weeks, and SEO/GEO campaigns are structured as ongoing monthly engagements.

What is GEO optimization and why does it matter?

GEO optimization (Geographic Optimization) ensures your website ranks for location-specific searches and multi-language audiences. This includes local SEO, hreflang implementation, region-specific content, and Google Business Profile optimization — critical for businesses targeting specific geographic markets.

Do you offer white-label development for agencies?

Yes, absolutely. We offer full white-label development services for SEO agencies and digital studios. All deliverables are under your brand, completely confidential — your clients never know we exist unless you tell them.

What does "SEO-first development" actually mean?

It means SEO is built into the foundation of every website — not added as an afterthought. This includes semantic HTML, proper heading hierarchy, schema markup, optimized meta tags, page speed, mobile-first design, crawlability, and hreflang — all handled before launch.

What results can I expect from SEO services?

Results depend on your market, competition, and starting point. Generally, clients see meaningful keyword ranking improvements within 2–3 months, significant traffic growth within 4–6 months, and strong ROI within 6–12 months of consistent optimization.

How do you improve Core Web Vitals and page speed?

We run a comprehensive Lighthouse audit, optimize and convert images to WebP/AVIF, implement lazy loading, eliminate render-blocking resources, minimize JavaScript bundles, and fix CLS/LCP/FID issues systematically. Most sites reach 90+ Lighthouse scores after our optimization.

What is your pricing structure?

Pricing is project-based and varies by scope. Landing pages start from $800, full websites from $2,500, and ongoing SEO/GEO retainers from $600/month. We provide transparent fixed-price proposals after an initial discovery call — no hidden fees, ever.
